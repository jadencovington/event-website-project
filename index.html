Dive into Wonder

Explore the mysteries of the underwater world in a fun, educational, and immersive science festival within New York City

Our Story

Dive into the captivating world of marine life at our annual Marine Biology Festival. This extraordinary event offers an immersive and educational experience for all ages. Explore interactive exhibits featuring marine specimens, engage with passionate marine biologists during expert talks, and embark on guided tours of nearby marine habitats. The festival also showcases marine-inspired art, raises awareness about conservation efforts, and provides family-friendly activities. Join us for a day of adventure, culinary delights, and a deeper understanding of the beauty and importance of our oceans. Whether you're a seasoned marine enthusiast or just curious about the underwater world, the Marine Biology Festival is a must-attend event. Come, be inspired, and deepen your connection with the marine realm, celebrating the wonders of our oceans.

Science Trivia

Test your marine knowledge at our thrilling Science Trivia Challenge! Dive deep into the world of oceanography, marine biology, and conservation as you compete against fellow enthusiasts. Join us for a night of fun and friendly competition, where you can learn fascinating facts about our oceans and their inhabitants.

Friday, September 29th

During this special day of SUBMERGE, teachers can book a field trip to attend the festival. Students will have the opportunity to participate in this interactive event and try out jaw-dropping experiments, meet local wildlife, and learn from local STEM experts.

Saturday, September 30th

And then there’s fun for all ages during the free public festival on Saturday, September 30. Join us to celebrate NYC’s local waterways at SUBMERGE during this extraordinary showcase of river wildlife and hands-on science activities!

Welcome to the marine biology festival, nestled at the edge of Manhattan in the picturesque Hudson River Park. This unique location offers festival-goers a captivating blend of natural beauty and urban vibrancy. As you explore the wonders of the deep, you'll be treated to breathtaking views of the Hudson River and the iconic Manhattan skyline as your backdrop. It's a truly one-of-a-kind experience, where you can immerse yourself in the world of marine life while savoring the dynamic energy of New York City. Whether you're a marine enthusiast, a science lover, or simply seeking a delightful day out, this location offers the perfect fusion of aquatic exploration and metropolitan excitement. Join us in this extraordinary setting at the heart of the Big Apple, where you'll embark on a marine adventure like no other.
